Pocket Projects

All prices in Canadian Dollars. Shipping is calculated in the next step.

Our pocket projects are designed to be, well, pocket-sized! These projects are small and portable enough to be brought along and worked on wherever you have time to work on them. Expect a greater emphasis on hand-sewing and on more decorative pieces with some small functional items.

Examples of projects you might find in a pocket project subscription are fabric bookmarks, keychains, pouches, or patches. All pocket projects can still contain a mix of needlework skills. While specific projects might focus more heavily on just embroidery or just appliqué, this subscription will still allow you to explore the full world of needlework.
