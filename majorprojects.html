Our major projects are for those dedicated crafters who want to sit down and create something significant. These projects are bigger and more involved, access to a sewing machine is not necessary, but could be helpful for some major projects.

Examples of projects you might receive with a major project subscription are quilts, garments, bags, or large embroidery or cross-stitch artworks. While all projects will come with all the materials and instruction you need regardless of skill level, if you've never tried any needlework before, we recommend giving our pocket projects a try before taking on our major projects.
